About This Project

This is a poject done by 1st year students of Chitkara University Rajpura Campus. The project was given by the IWT professor Dr. Leema Nelson for the marks assessment for 1st year. The project was made by a team of 4 members each working day and night to create this project. The original idea was of the team leader Ayush Goyal, the main motto was to create a user friendly environment where the users can manage their expenses easily and can grow their money as well as themselves.

Our Motto

The whole and sole motto of our team while creating this project was to provide our users a friendly and secure environment, where they can manage and monitor their expenses and save themselves from unknown expenses and being extravagent. The exact motto is still under work but the current motto is

"MANAGE YOUR EXPENSES,
WITH OUR EXPERTISE"
